HiStack Guide: Design

Author

HiStack administration

Share

Introduction

Welcome to the HiStack Design Guide! If you're looking to create a visually appealing and functional design for your project, this guide will help you define the key guidelines before we begin our collaboration. Well-prepared information speeds up the design process and ensures that the final result matches your needs and vision.

1. Defining Visual Identity

Before we begin the design work, it’s important that you have a basic idea of the visual identity you want to achieve:

  • 1. What is the main purpose and goal of the design?
  • 2. What emotions and messages do you want to convey to users?
  • 3. Do you have an existing brand identity that should be integrated into the design?

A clearly defined identity makes branding more consistent and recognizable.

2. Color Palette and Typography

Colors and fonts play a key role in shaping visual identity:

  • 1. Choose primary colors that will dominate the design.
  • 2. Define secondary colors to be used for accents.
  • 3. Consider font styles that reflect the brand’s tone and purpose.

If you're unsure, we can help you choose based on best practices and industry standards.

3. Design Tone and Aesthetics

The visual tone of the design should reflect your brand's values and message:

  • 1. Do you want a modern, minimalist, luxurious, or classic design?
  • 2. Should it be serious and professional or relaxed and creative?
  • 3. What style of illustrations, icons, and graphics fits you best?

Choosing the right tone helps create a consistent user experience.

4. Inspiration and Reference Examples

Sometimes the easiest way to communicate your vision is through concrete examples:

  • 1. Send links to websites, apps, or designs you like.
  • 2. Create a moodboard with visual elements that inspire you.
  • 3. Describe what you like about specific examples and how you'd like us to implement it.

Reference materials help designers better understand your preferences.

5. Functionality and User Experience

In addition to aesthetics, the design must be functional and user-oriented:

  • 1. What are the key features the design should support?
  • 2. Are there any specific UX/UI requirements?
  • 3. How do you want users to interact with the design?

A well-thought-out UX leads to higher user engagement and better conversion.

6. Responsiveness and Screen Adaptation

Modern design must be optimized for various devices:

  • 1. Is the design primarily intended for desktop, mobile devices, or both?
  • 2. Are there specific dimensions or resolutions that need to be considered?
  • 3. How should the interface behave at different screen sizes?

We ensure every design performs flawlessly across all platforms.

7. Finalization and Feedback

Once the design is created, we proceed with the review and revision process:

  • 1. We send you the first version for review.
  • 2. We receive your feedback and make the necessary adjustments.
  • 3. Once approved, we prepare the final files in the agreed formats.

Your feedback is essential to achieving the perfect final result.

8. Branding and Logo Design

If you don't have a defined visual identity, we can help you create a logo and branding strategy. Our process begins with market and competitor research to understand what will set you apart. Then, we create a unique logo that reflects your brand values and is tailored to your target audience.

After the logo is created, we develop a complete visual identity including colors, typography, and style guidelines to ensure consistency across all materials.

9. User Interface Design (UI/UX)

For digital projects, it's crucial that the design is intuitive and user-friendly. We start by analyzing user behavior to understand their needs and challenges. Based on this, we create wireframes and prototypes that visualize interactions before starting the final design.

After implementation, we test the design and iteratively improve it based on feedback, ensuring the final solution is functional and visually appealing.

Conclusion

Good design starts with a clear vision and communication. By following this guide, you can define key design aspects in advance and simplify the creation process. Our team is here to turn your ideas into a visually striking and functional solution.

If you're ready to get started, contact us and together we will create a design that will make your brand stand out!
